Pune District Education Association :

Pune District Education Association (PDEA) is well known educational society founded by the dedicated team of social reformers lead by Late Shri. Baburaoji Gholap and has a standing of over 77 years in the field of education. PDEA has received the prestigious, “Best Educational Institute Award” in the year 2000 by the Government of Maharashtra for the remarkable achievements in the field of education and social work. PDEA imparts education to rural and urban masses through Preprimary Schools (12), Primary Schools (13) Secondary Schools (55), Subsidiary School (06), Higher Secondary Schools (29), Senior Colleges (08), Ayurved College and Research Center (01), Pharmacy Colleges (02), Management Institutes (02), Polytechnic Institute (01), Engineering College (01), Law College (01), Architecture (01) and D.Ed. College (01). In the year 2006, inspired visionary and dynamic leader, Honorable Shri. Ajit Pawar took the charge as President of PDEA. Under his able guidance, all branches of PDEA’s are now taking a global speed to reach the destination.

About Baburaoji Gholap College :

Baburaoji Gholap College runs 16 undergraduate courses and 11 postgraduate courses under the faculties of Arts, Commerce, Science, Computer Science and Computer Application. In addition to conventional courses, there are 5 B.Voc. Degree courses in Fashion Technology, Software Development, Beauty Therapy and Aesthetics, Retail Management and Travel and Tourism Management. College also offers ‘Diploma in Rubber Chemistry and Processing’ under UGC Community College Scheme. There are four Ph.D. Research centers in Economics, Physics, Chemistry and Environmental Science wherein 9 have been awarded with Ph.D. since 2012 and 106 students are currently pursuing their doctoral degree. An excellent ‘Sophisticated Instrumental Analysis Facility Centre (SIAFC)’ Laboratory for research activities is set up. The college received general development assistance from UGC, New Delhi and also funded with DBT-Star college scheme and DST-FIST scheme. Recently, college is selected for Unnant Bharat Abhiyan Scheme of UGC, New Delhi.

The college is recipient of the ‘Best College Award’ by Savitribai Phule Pune University in 2010; and first prize for “Jagar Janivancha” activity at University level by Government of Maharashtra in 2013 and 2014.

Scope of The Conference :

In era of 21st century, advanced materials have shown tremendous potential for revolution in Science and Technology. Establishing the sustainable materials at scientific, technological and industrial levels needs the encroachment of fundamental and extrinsic knowledge from all fields of science. An interdisciplinary approach might be useful in understanding the various aspects of progresses in the sustainable materials. In view of this, the present international conference will be encompassing the expert guidance from distinguished academician, researchers, scientists and industrialist in the area of sustainable materials. The conference will give channel to share the ideas and views regarding the research outcomes in sustainable materials through the invited talk, oral presentation and poster presentation. The main objectives of the conference are:
1. To convene academicians and researchers from various colleges, universities, research institutes and multinational industries from the fields of sustainable materials.
2. To render a platform to research scholars and academicians for presenting, sharing and exchanging their research outcomes and ideas in the field of sustainable materials.
3. To create awareness about the recent developments in the field of sustainable materials.
4. To explore career opportunities and future scope in an interdisciplinary field of sustainable materials.

About Pune City :

Pune is eighth largest metropolitan in Maharashtra state of India. Pune was once the center of power of Maratha Empire and the first capital of the great Maratha king Chatrapatti Shivaji Maharaj. It is center of knowledge and hence often known as the Oxford of the east. The students from different states and also from various countries are completing their higher and doctoral education in field science, engineering medical and technology at different educational institutes and universities on Pune. Pune is having more than hundred educational institutes and nine universities. Pune has various government institutes like NCL, C-MET, TIFR, NCRA, IUCAA, IISER, DRDO, IITM, NIV, NIC, HERML, TRDDC etc for higher education and advance research. Pune also known as cultural capital of Maharashtra and there are many historic places like Shaniwar Wada, Lal Mahal and many museums like the Aga Khan Palace, Raja Kelkar Museum, National War museum, etc. The weather of Pune is very pleasant.
